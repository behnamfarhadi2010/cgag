Our Mission

The Coast Guard Alumni Art Gallery is dedicated to preserving and celebrating the rich artistic heritage and maritime history of the Coast Guard through a unique collection of artwork created by alumni. Our gallery showcases 74 exceptional pieces that capture the spirit of service, the beauty of our coasts, and the courage of those who protect our waters.

Our Collection

Each artwork in our collection tells a unique story from the perspective of Coast Guard servicemen and women. From dramatic rescues to serene lighthouses, from stormy seas to peaceful harbors, our collection represents the multifaceted experience of Coast Guard service. The artists featured in our gallery include retired admirals, active-duty personnel, and veterans whose service spans decades of Coast Guard history. Their artistic skills and personal experiences combine to create a powerful visual testimony to the Coast Guard motto: Semper Paratus (Always Ready).
