Bacalhao Island Lighthouse

Bacalhao Island Lighthouse

December 6, 1894 by Keeper William Howell

About the Artwork

A stunning portrayal of the Cape May Lighthouse at dawn, with the first rays of sunlight breaking through storm clouds. Captain Stevens painted this piece after serving 20 years in the Coast Guard, capturing the beauty and symbolism of lighthouses as beacons of hope and safety.

Medium

Oil on canvas

Year

1987
